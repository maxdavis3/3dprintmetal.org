CAPABILITIES

Research-Driven Capabilities

01

Process Development

DMLS parameter optimization: laser power, scan speed, layer thickness, hatch spacing — fine-tuned for each alloy to achieve maximum density and mechanical properties.

02

Alloy Characterization

Tensile testing to ASTM E8, Rockwell hardness (B/C scale), SEM microstructure analysis, and XRD phase identification for research-grade material data.

03

Industrial Transfer

Translating research insights into production processes. From lab-scale parameter studies to ISO-certified production runs for aerospace and medical OEMs.
